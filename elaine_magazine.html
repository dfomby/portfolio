Elaine Lustig Cohen Magazine

The content of this magazine is from a bibliography about the designer Elaine Lustig Cohen. Dummy text was added as well to complete the layout design.

Ideation

Design Style:
‚ùñ Lively, Playful yet Cohesive
The design style should be unique and eye-catching, but still have a structured formatting. The lively and playful design style communicates the type of design style that the designer used in her works. 2 of her designs that reflect this design style will be used in the magazine.

Results

This logo was created using Adobe InDesign.

Magazine Cover:

For the front page of the magazine, I incorporated a picture of Elaine Cohen embedded in a circle that's cutting off the page, in order to convey her picture in a lively and playful style.

Inside Pages 2-3:

In order to properly convey Elaine Cohen's design style, I decided that I would use images for each page embedded in a cut-off circle. I used the gestalt idea of closure, as even though the circles are cut off on the pages, the reader is able to connect the circle lines mentally as if they were continuous. These circles also follow the principle of continuance as the strokes for the circles continue across pages 2-3. I decided that the the text should wrap around the circle designs I made to create a playful effect, conveying Elaine Cohen's design style. The images I used were designs made by Elaine Cohen, and the images have the same matching colors, creating a color scheme for pages 2-3. The borders of the circles are blue to connect to the front page's blue colors scheme.
