Solvoltic

Solvoltic is a start-up green energy firm located in the Bronx. They sell solar panels that people can install on their own and use on their balconies. They enable more people to invest in and use solar energy to power home electronics.

Potential Users/Clients:
People looking to invest in solar energy to power their homes.
People looking to use an eco-friendly alternative that's easy to set up.

The company’s purpose: Enable people to invest in solar energy.
The company needs to have an ideal logo that both sticks out and represents their purpose.

Researching the purpose of solar panels:
❖ Reduce reliance on fossil fuels (eco-friendly alternative)
❖ Generating electricity for households, reducing electricity costs overtime

This logo was created using Adobe Illustrator

To make the logo both stand out and convey the company's purpose, I decided that the logo should consist of both a solar panel and a leaf. Seeing the solar panel will make potential customers instantly aware that this is a solar panel company. Since most people associate the symbol of a leaf with eco-friendly, I placed a leaf directly in the middle of the logo, making it stand out as much as possible, so that even at a glance customers can tell that this product will be eco-friendly.
